This text clarifies the source of the icons to be found in this folder and the licenses that apply
on them. The icons in this folder can be divided into three categories:

    STREAMLINE ICONS (SLI):
        These are the icons downloaded directly from StreamlineHQ:
        https://www.streamlinehq.com/icons/streamline-flat-pro
        We have 94 such icons.

        Webalys LLC is the company that has created the Streamline Icons for personal and commercial
        use. I, Kristof Mulier, purchased the premium license in August 2022 to access and use up to
        100 icons from Webalys LLC in a commercial project. As cofounder of Embeetle, I decided to
        use these icons in the Embeetle software and on the Embeetle website.
        In exchange for the purchase, Webalys LLC grants to me, Kristof Mulier, the Customer, a
        license under the copyrights to use Streamline assets in accordance with their terms and
        conditions. For these terms and conditions, I refer to their website:
        https://www.streamlinehq.com/icons

        You, the user of Embeetle IDE, are not allowed to use these icons in your own project,
        unless you contact Webalys LLC and pay for the premium license.

    MODIFIED ICONS (MOD):
        These are the icons derived from one or more of the 94 downloaded streamline icons mentioned
        above. We have 313 such modified icons. The license on these icons is the same as the one on
        the original streamline icons.
        You, the user of Embeetle IDE, are not allowed to use these icons in your own project,
        unless you contact Webalys LLC and pay for the premium license.

    SELF DESIGNED ICON (EMB):
        These icons are designed from scratch by me, Kristof Mulier, intended for the Embeetle soft-
        ware and Embeetle website. We have 135 such icons. I release these icons under the CC BY 4.0
        license as described here:
        https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode
        This means you can copy, adapt, and redistribute the material in any medium or format, as
        long as you give appropriate credit to Embeetle and provide a link to the license.
